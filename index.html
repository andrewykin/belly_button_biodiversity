Roza is interested in bacterial species that have the ability to synthesize proteins that taste like beef. Her lab has partnered with Improbable Beef, a food startup to research candidate species. Roza hypothesizes that a microorganism found in the belly button can supply the best taste for artifical beef. After sampling the belly buttons of people around the country, Roza wants to build a dashboard that participants and researchers can access.
